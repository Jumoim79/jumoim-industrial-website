\# RFQ Submission Guide



To start your project with JUMOIM, please provide:



\## Required Files



\- 3D CAD file (STEP / IGES / STL preferred)

\- Technical drawings (if available)

\- Material requirements

\- Quantity (prototype / batch / mass production)



\## Optional but helpful



\- Surface finish requirements

\- Tolerance specifications

\- Application industry



\## What happens next



1\. Engineering review (DFM analysis)

2\. Manufacturing process recommendation

3\. Quotation

4\. Production scheduling



\## Important



Incomplete information may delay quotation.



👉 Upload complete data for fastest response.
