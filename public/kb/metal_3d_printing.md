\# Metal 3D Printing (Additive Manufacturing)



JUMOIM offers industrial metal 3D printing for complex geometries that cannot be achieved with traditional machining.



\## Technology



\- Selective Laser Melting (SLM)

\- Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS)

\- Metal additive hybrid manufacturing

\- Post-machining CNC finishing integration



\## Materials



\- Stainless steel (316L, 17-4PH)

\- Titanium alloys (Ti6Al4V)

\- Aluminum alloys

\- Nickel-based superalloys (Inconel)



\## Key Advantages



\- Complex internal structures

\- Lightweight lattice design

\- Conformal cooling channels

\- Reduced assembly parts count

\- Rapid prototyping to functional production



\## Conformal Cooling Mold Advantage
